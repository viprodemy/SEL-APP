# **App Name**: SEL Assistant

## Core Features:

- Emotion Check-in: Allow students to select their current emotion and intensity. AI tracks the emotion trends over time. Records PSL emoji.
- Descriptive Input: Record student voice or text descriptions of events triggering emotions. AI can analyze keywords.
- Body Scan Input: Prompt students to identify physical sensations associated with their emotions.
- Expectation Input: Guide students to articulate expectations related to the feeling, and needs unmet.
- Emotion Decoder: AI asks guiding questions based on the student's input. The AI will function as a tool to assist the student in uncovering the purpose and needs associated with each emotion.
- Cool-Down Zone: Offer guided breathing exercises, body scans, and a safe imagination exercise.
- NLP Reframing: AI provides reframing options/affirmations related to the selected emotion, including 3 language selection.
- Summary Dashboard: Visualize growth and insights for students, teachers, and parents via automated charts/summaries.
- Reward System: Integrate check-in frequency rewards tied to a growth garden concept with badges.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: HSL 214, 95%, 51% (Hex: #039BE5), a bright vibrant blue. A vibrant color to express the urgency of expressing ones emotion.
- Background color: HSL 214, 20%, 90% (Hex: #D6EAF8), a light tint of blue. A bright color to express the urgency of expressing ones emotion.
- Accent color: HSL 184, 61%, 44% (Hex: #26A69A), a desaturated, but bright cyan. Complementary to the primary, while offering additional expression to the users selections.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif
- Use simple, friendly icons for each emotion category.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout to promote focus.
- Subtle animations when selecting emotions, completing exercises, or earning rewards.
